01
Penn State 2026 - Present

Industrial Time-Series Foundation Models

Developing transferable representations for industrial sensor data under changing operating conditions, irregular observations, and limited downstream labels.

  • Language-aligned contrastive pretraining connects sensor signals with operating-condition semantics for zero- and few-shot transfer.
  • Condition-Quotient Attention (CQuAT) estimates a low-rank condition-change subspace from paired observations and removes nuisance directions before self-attention.
  • Held-out condition splits, subspace recovery diagnostics, and targeted ablations directly test invariance and transfer claims.
Foundation models Contrastive learning Subspace methods Domain generalization

Research collaboration 2026 - Present

Constraint-Aware PCB Placement and Routing with LLM Agents

Contributing evaluation and tool-augmented experiment components to OmniLayout, a schematic-coupled benchmark of 1,681 PCB layouts spanning geometric legality, routability, and electrical functionality.

  • Designed a current-routing score that combines connectivity, routing-only design rule violations, efficiency, and prerouting preservation.
  • Built iterative LLM routing agents and tool-access ablations to isolate the value of visualization, scoring, semantic guidance, and partial-route updates.
LLM agents Multimodal reasoning Constraint evaluation
