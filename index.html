PhD Researcher in Electrical Engineering

Haolin Ye

The Pennsylvania State University, State College, Pennsylvania

I study time-series foundation models and reliable representation learning. My current work asks how pretrained temporal models can transfer across domains, adapt efficiently, and remain robust under missing data, irregular sampling, and distribution shift.

Email LinkedIn GitHub

Research interests

  • Time-series foundation models. Cross-domain pretraining and transferable temporal representations.
  • Multi-scale sequence modeling. Adaptive patching, frequency-aware features, and long-range dynamics.
  • Efficient adaptation. Parameter-efficient methods and compact inference for temporal models.
  • Reliable learning. Generalization under distribution shift, missing observations, and limited labels.
